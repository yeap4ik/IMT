Roccat kone

The ROCCAT Kone Pro Air is a high-performance wireless gaming mouse designed for speed, comfort, and precision. Featuring ROCCAT’s Titan Switch Optical technology, it delivers ultra-fast actuation with long-lasting durability rated for 100 million clicks. The mouse is equipped with a 19,000 DPI Owl-Eye optical sensor for pinpoint accuracy, and its lightweight design—just 75 grams—ensures effortless movement during intense gameplay. The Kone Pro Air supports both 2.4GHz wireless and Bluetooth connectivity, offering versatility for gaming and everyday use, with up to 100 hours of battery life on a single charge. Its ergonomic shape, translucent heat-treated shell, and customizable AIMO RGB lighting make it a stylish and comfortable choice for gamers who demand both form and function.
